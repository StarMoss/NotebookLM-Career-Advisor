# Observations about LLMs used for this project

## ChatGPT

### Pros

- Strong at synthesizing information from prior conversations (system design, troubleshooting, how-tos)
- Effective for quick, straightforward Q&A

### Cons

- Can be overly sycophantic
- Sometimes fabricates details or makes unsupported assumptions
- May project more confidence than warranted

## Gemini

### Pros

- Integrates with Google Drive and can leverage existing documents

### Cons

- Tone can feel stiff and unnatural
- Requires very specific prompting to get desired results
- Uncreative

## NotebookLM

### Pros

- Uses only uploaded, vetted content
- Supports a wide range of file types
- Can generate video, audio, images, and documents from source material
- Keeps information private and contained

### Cons

- Limited to provided content; lacks external perspective

## Claude

### Pros

- Most natural, human-like language
- Strong nuance and tone
- High-quality document formatting

## Cons

- Expensive
