# Example Prompts

*Prompts for generating career insight in LLMs.*

Based on our past conversations and the work we've discussed, please create a comprehensive professional profile of me. Include the following:

- My strongest technical skills and areas of expertise
- Technologies, tools, and platforms I appear proficient in
- The types of problems I tend to work on and solve
- The role I seem to play on projects (for example: implementer, architect, troubleshooter, leader, etc.)
- The level of seniority my work reflects and your reasoning
- Examples of the kinds of impact or value I appear to provide
- My strengths compared to typical engineers
- Any patterns you notice in how I approach problems or systems
Please be specific and base your answer on actual examples from our conversations where possible. Present this as an objective, neutral professional assessment.

Based on my current skills and experience, what additional skills would most increase my effectiveness or career growth?

Based on my work and responsibilities, what career trajectory does my experience seem to be following?

What accomplishments or contributions from my work appear most significant and why?

What evidence suggests staff-level or architect-level capability, if any?
